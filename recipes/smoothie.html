Banana Strawberry Blueberry Smoothie

This is an amazing tasting smoothie! It only has the essentials, it has no added unneeded Ingridents such as sugar, milk, etc. The combination of the strawberries, blueberries, and banana gives it a very sweet taste with an explosion of the mix of fruit flavors. This is a smoothie to try.

Ingridents:

  • 2 bananas
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 8 strawberries (hulled and stems removed)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Recipe makes (2) 12 ounce cups.
